Project Overview
You are developing an AI‑enabled aircraft refueling monitoring system that continuously evaluates fuel quality, equipment health, and operational safety using data from multiple sensors. The system integrates AI algorithms with measurements such as flow, pressure, differential pressure, water contamination, vibration, tank level, and grounding status. 

🎯 Objectives
Provide real‑time decision support during refueling.
Enable predictive maintenance to detect equipment failures early.
Implement intelligent safety interlocks using AI.
Improve operational safety through AI‑driven insights.
Maintain a digital monitoring platform that records all refueling parameters for traceability, audits, and incident analysis. 
🛠️ System Features
Your system integrates data from multiple sensors, including:
Fuel flow sensors
Pressure & differential pressure sensors
Water contamination sensors
Tank level sensors
Temperature sensors
Brake interlock sensors
Platform wand sensors
Positioning sensors or cameras
AI processes this data to deliver:
Real‑time fuel quality monitoring
Equipment health diagnostics
Abnormality detection
Predictive maintenance
Intelligent safety interlocks
Real‑time operational alerts
Decision support for operators
